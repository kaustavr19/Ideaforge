# IdeaForge

IdeaForge is a React-based workspace designed for personal ideas, bringing together task management, note-taking, and productivity tracking with a gamified twist. It provides a flexible and visually appealing environment to take your ideas from concept to completion.

## Features

* **Gamified Productivity:** Earn XP, level up, and unlock achievements as you complete tasks, write notes, and create projects.
* **Task Management:** Organize your work with multiple views:
  * **Kanban Board:** Visually track task progress across different statuses (Idea, Planned, In Progress, Blocked, Done).
  * **List View:** A compact overview of all your tasks.
  * **Timeline View:** Plan your tasks on a timeline based on start and due dates.
* **Rich Note-Taking:** Write notes with Markdown support. Use a formatting toolbar or quick slash commands (`/h1`, `/todo`, `/quote`, etc.) for fast and easy formatting.
* **Customizable Environments:** Personalize your workspace:
  * Toggle between **Light** and **Dark** modes.
  * Choose from distinct "Atmospheres" (Ocean, Aurora, Minimal).
  * Assign colors to projects to easily distinguish tasks visually.
* **Dashboard & Insights:** A dedicated dashboard view gives you a quick breakdown of your stats, unlocked achievements, and current level progress.
* **Project Organization:** Group tasks and notes by projects to keep your ideas structured.

## Technologies Used

* Built with React (Hooks: `useState`, `useEffect`, `useRef`, `useMemo`).
* Custom lightweight CSS for styling and animations (Bricolage Grotesque, Manrope, and JetBrains Mono fonts).
* Markdown parsing for rich text rendering.
